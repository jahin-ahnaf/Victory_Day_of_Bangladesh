The Starting

At first, our country was known as the East Pakistan cause Pakistan was devided by 2 parts. One the East Pakistan and the other one the West Pakistan. From the beginning of devition in 1947, West Pakistan was cruel to East Pakistan cause the capital city was in West Pakistan. They (the west pakistan) started to take our independence from our language Bangla. They wanted to make Urdu the language of Pakistan and destroy Bangla.

Starting of language movement

When Bangalis couldn't take enough, then they took movement against West Pakistan (today's pakistan). On 21th February in 1952, the Bangalis took movement. But the Pakistan government announced 144. But they couldn't stop the Bangalis! Polices shooted on them. About a lot of people died in this day such as Rafiq, Jabbar, Shafiur etc. 21st February is now the international mother language day.

The Liberation War
